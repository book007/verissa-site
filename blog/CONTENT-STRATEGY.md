# Verissa Blog — SEO Content Strategy

## Mission
A premium hospitality growth publication designed to attract Italian hotel owners and convert them into demo requests.

Every article moves the reader toward: **"Guarda la trasformazione del tuo hotel."**

---

## CORE RULE — Content Research (MANDATORY)

**Every single blog post MUST be based on real queries from actual hotel owners.** No invented topics. No guessing.

Before writing any article, research real search demand from:
1. **Google "People Also Ask"** — search the target keyword in Italian Google and extract PAA questions
2. **Google Autocomplete** — type the seed keyword and capture what Google suggests
3. **Hotel industry forums and Q&A sites** — FindQuestions.com, AlsoAsked.com, AnswerThePublic, or similar Italian-language equivalents
4. **Italian hospitality communities** — forums, Facebook groups, Reddit threads where Italian hoteliers discuss problems

The article topic, H2 headings, and FAQ section must map directly to real queries found during research. Document the source queries in the article planning phase.

## Internal Linking Rules

1. Every article must contain **2-4 internal links** to related articles
2. Every article must link to at least **1 article in a different cluster**
3. Every article must have a **"Continua a leggere"** section with 3 related articles
4. Every article ends with **"Guarda la trasformazione del tuo hotel"** CTA
5. Pillar pages link to ALL articles in their cluster
6. Articles link UP to their pillar page

---

## Article Structure Template

Every article follows this structure:

1. **Hook** — Emotional opening (pain, frustration, recognition)
2. **Financial/Emotional Consequence** — What it costs to ignore this
3. **Why Most Hotels Fail** — Common mistakes, data
4. **Modern Solution** — What works, practical steps
5. **Subtle Verissa Positioning** — Results, examples, social proof
6. **Inline CTA** — "Guarda la trasformazione del tuo hotel"
7. **Actionable Steps** — 3 things to do today
8. **FAQ** — 5-7 questions for SEO rich snippets
9. **Final Transformation CTA** — Emotional close
10. **Related Articles** — 3 internal links

## Tone Guidelines

- Premium, not corporate
- Editorial, not marketing
- Practical, not theoretical
- Italian hospitality warmth, not cold agency speak
- Data-backed, not opinion-based
- Calm confidence, not aggressive selling

**Voice:** An experienced hospitality consultant sharing insights over coffee, not a salesperson pitching.
